Learn a little Thai!

I'm Thai-American, born and raised in Ann Arbor. I grew up speaking Thai only at home until I went to school. Here are some Thai words you can learn!
Hew (หิว)
Hungry. The -ew sound is short
Hnang Suu (หนังสือ)
Book. Hnang has a high sound, suu has a high and throaty sound.
Rien (เรียน)
Learn. Pronounced ree-en. The tone is even.
